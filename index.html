About Us

Family-Owned

  • 1984-1992

    The Idea

    Bob Koutoulogenis, a 1992 Stagg High School graduate who lives in Lemont with his wife and three children, has worked in restaurants since age 12.

  • 1998-2017

    Origins of Fork & Spoon

    His father, Nick Koutoulogenis, was an original partner at L&M Starlight Restaurant at 83rd and Kedzie in Chicago.

  • June 2018

    Grand Opening

    When his father retired, Bob became a partner and then he sold his interest in the southwest side eatery to open Fork & Spoon in Lemont.

Caramel Sleigh Latte

*Seasonal

A festive blend of rich espresso, velvety steamed milk, and smooth caramel syrup, finished with a hint of sea salt for the perfect balance of sweet and savory. Topped with whipped cream and a drizzle of caramel, this warm, indulgent drink will fill you with holiday cheer and the cozy spirit of Christmas!

Holiday Kolaczki Bites

*Seasonal

Delicate, buttery pastries filled with options for sweet fruit preserves like raspberry, apricot, or strawberry. These festive treats are lightly dusted with powdered sugar, offering a perfect balance of sweetness and texture. Traditionally enjoyed during the holiday season, Kolaczki are a delightful, melt-in-your-mouth treat that brings warmth and joy to every Christmas celebration.

Famous Hot Cocoa

*Seasonal

A rich, creamy drink made with smooth, melted chocolate and steamed milk, creating the ultimate indulgence for the holiday season. Topped with fluffy whipped cream, a sprinkle of cinnamon or peppermint, and maybe a few marshmallows, this cozy beverage is perfect for sipping by the fire, spreading festive cheer, and warming you up on chilly Christmas mornings.

North Pole Pancakes

*Seasonal

A fun and festive breakfast treat perfect for Christmas morning. These fluffy pancakes are topped with bananas to create Santa’s beard, fresh raspberries for his hat, and blueberries for his eyes, giving them a playful and jolly appearance. Finished with a drizzle of maple syrup, these pancakes make for a delightful holiday-inspired meal that will bring smiles to both kids and adults alike!

Raindeer Flapjacks

*Seasonal

A fun and festive breakfast that brings the magic of Christmas to your table. These fluffy pancakes are decorated with blueberries for eyes, a strawberry for a red Rudolph nose, and bacon for antlers, creating an adorable reindeer face. Drizzled with syrup and served with a sprinkle of powdered sugar, these playful pancakes are a delightful treat to kick off the holiday season!

Jolly Veggie Scramble

*Seasonal

A healthy and vibrant way to start your holiday morning. Packed with a colorful mix of roasted or sautéed veggies like spinach, avocado, tomatoes, and potatoes, this bowl is topped with a poached egg or sausage for a protein boost. Seasoned with festive herbs like rosemary and thyme, it's a flavorful and nourishing dish that will energize you for a day of Christmas celebrations, offering a savory, wholesome alternative to the usual holiday sweets!
